Hello Folks!

To start, I am fascinated by books, architecture, and plants. I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon. Yes, a true Oregonian. I love my trees. I have always had an eye for fashion. You are beautiful inside and out. Do not let anyone ever take that away from you.

Yours truly, Julie Alvarez.
